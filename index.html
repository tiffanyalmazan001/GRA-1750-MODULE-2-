Gluten free pumpkin muffins

It’s officially pumpkin season and I for one am so excited. Pumpkin bread, pumpkin cookies, pumpkin pie and these new gluten free pumpkin muffins! They’re perfect for a quick snack or dessert and I promise you’d never tell they’re both gluten and dairy free.

Ingredients for pumpkin pie muffin

Wet ingredients
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 6 tablespoons Butter
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla & 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cups Pumpkin puree
    • Dry ingredients
  • 2/3 cup White sugar
  • 1/2 cup light Brown sugar
  • 1 3/4 cup Gluten free flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon soda & 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup Chocolate chips
  • 1/1 teaspoon cinnamon

    • Ingredients for Streusel topping

  • 4 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons gluten free flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 tablespoon pumpkin pie spice

    • Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Line a muffin tin with 12 liners and set aside.
  • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together all wet ingredients until smooth.
  • Whisk in the dry ingredients making sure to spoon and dlevel the flour.
  • Fold in the chocolate chips (optional)
  • Scoop the batter into a muffin tray, filing each to the top to ensure nice round muffin tops.
  • For the stresuel topping: In a small dish, combine the sugar, flour and pumpkin pie spice. Cut in the butter with a fork until crumbly
  • Use your fingers to sprinkle the streusel on top of the muffins. Bake for 28-32 minutes.
  • Let cool for about 15 minutes in the tray then remove and transfer to a wire rack to finish cooling
  • Enjoy!!!!
